Welcome to Asenter Invest

“EXCELLENCE IN SECOND HAND CLOTHES ON TIME, EVERY TIME”

 

ABOUT US

Asenter Invest is committed to running a socially responsible business. We are quickly establishing ourselves as the ultimate reference in the EU’s Textile Reuse industry. We operate various clothing collection schemes aimed at diverting unwanted clothing from EU landfill for the purpose of reuse.

Today we are recognised as one of the EU’s leading suppliers of quality second hand clothing with a strong presence in the European market.

 

OUR SPECIALITY

The clothing includes a mix of Ladies, Menswear, Kids, Shoes in pairs, Fashion Accessories, Bedlinen, Soft toys, Cosmetics and Toiletries. Our company specialises in Unsorted Original second hand clothing and Bric-a-brac.

 

OUR QUALITY

We pride ourselves on the genuine quality of product we offer. Our customers can be assured the quality of our Unsorted Original is constantly maintained to the highest standards. We remove all wet/soiled/damaged items to ensure that we meet our customers’ expectations.
